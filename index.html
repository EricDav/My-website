About Me

My story

I’m David Alienyi, a proud native of Enugu State, Nigeria, born and raised in the vibrant city of Lagos. With over seven years of experience as a Fullstack and Mobile Developer, I’ve cultivated a passion for solving problems—whether they’re mathematical, logical, or technical. This curiosity first drew me to programming at the age of 19, sparked by my elder brother, Daniel Alienyi, who introduced me to the craft after I completed high school. My journey began with Python, writing simple programs to tackle everyday challenges, and it’s been an exhilarating ride ever since.

Today, I’m a self-proclaimed PHP enthusiast, thriving on building robust web applications with PHP and JavaScript. I also dabble in Node.js to craft scalable APIs and lean toward Angular for sleek, client-side experiences. My interests extend to the fascinating world of AI, where I explore innovative solutions to complex problems. As a mobile developer, I’m equally at home creating native apps with Java or hybrid ones using Ionic, delivering seamless functionality across platforms.

Beyond code, I’m a lover of good music—genre boundaries don’t apply—and a devoted football fan, proudly cheering for Chelsea. I see myself as a problem-solver at heart, always eager to collaborate and turn ideas into impactful realities. Let’s connect and create something remarkable together!
